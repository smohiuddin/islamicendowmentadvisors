Our Mission

Why we exist

The Islamic Endowment Foundation helps mosques and Islamic organizations across the United States establish, grow, and advise endowment funds — so that generosity compounds across generations.

Our founders have built investment funds, chaired endowment boards, and spent years studying what it takes for these institutions to thrive. We exist to make that expertise accessible to every masjid and Islamic organization, regardless of size or resources.

No fees. No retainers. Just service.

"An endowment is not a balance sheet entry. It is a covenant with the generations that come after you."
